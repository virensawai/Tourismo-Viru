Our Journey

The Story Behind Tourismo

Tourismo was born from a simple idea: to make discovering Amravati's treasures easier and more engaging for everyone. As students and locals, we saw the incredible potential in our region's rich history, stunning natural landscapes, and vibrant culture.

Our Mission

We realized that while the passion to explore existed, a modern, centralized, and user-friendly guide was missing. That's why we, CEP-GROUP-12, decided to combine our skills in technology and design to build a platform that not only informs but also inspires.
