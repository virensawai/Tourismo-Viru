Bamboo Garden 

The Bamboo Garden in Amravati, India’s first dedicated bamboo park, offers a unique eco-tourism experience with over 65 species of bamboo and a specialized cactus garden featuring 300+ varieties. With scenic walking trails, bamboo bridges, and a peaceful lotus pond, it’s a perfect getaway for nature lovers, families, and photographers alike. Located at Forest Rest House, Wadali, Amravati Camp, this destination beautifully blends nature, learning, and relaxation.
