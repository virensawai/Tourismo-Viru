Amravati's Urban Oasis: Oxygen Park

Located in the heart of the city, Oxygen Park is a verdant sanctuary offering a much-needed escape from urban life. This beautifully landscaped space serves as a vital green lung for Amravati, providing a clean, pollution-free environment for relaxation, recreation, and rejuvenation for all.

Highlights & Key Features

A Breath of Fresh Air

The park is a haven of clean, fresh air, thanks to its dense plantation of oxygen-producing trees. It provides a crucial pollution-free zone where visitors can enjoy the well-maintained walking and jogging tracks.

Recreation for All Ages

Catering to everyone, the park includes a dedicated, safe playground for children, making it an ideal family destination. For adults, there are serene, open areas perfect for yoga and meditation.

Themed Botanical Gardens

Beyond its general greenery, Oxygen Park features several themed gardens that add to its charm. Visitors can explore beautiful lotus ponds, a tranquil bamboo grove, and a diverse cacti garden, offering a rich botanical experience.

Clean and Accessible

The park is known for its cleanliness and well-maintained facilities. Its accessible location and thoughtful design ensure a comfortable and pleasant visit for everyone, including families with young children and elderly visitors.

With its peaceful atmosphere and beautiful landscapes, Oxygen Park is a must-visit destination for anyone seeking a refreshing break and a moment of tranquility in Amravati.
