Bamboo Garden 

The Bamboo Garden in Amravati, India’s first dedicated bamboo park, offers a unique eco-tourism experience with over 65 species of bamboo and a specialized cactus garden featuring 300+ varieties. With scenic walking trails, bamboo bridges, and a peaceful lotus pond, it’s a perfect getaway for nature lovers, families, and photographers alike. Located at Forest Rest House, Wadali, Amravati Camp, this destination beautifully blends nature, learning, and relaxation.

2

Oxegen Park

The Oxygen Park in Amravati is a refreshing green sanctuary that promotes a healthy, pollution-free environment. Filled with diverse trees and plants, it naturally purifies the air while offering scenic trails, a children’s play area, and peaceful spots for yoga and meditation. Located at Old Panchvati, near Wadali Camp, Shyam Nagar, it’s an ideal destination for families, fitness enthusiasts, and nature seekers.

3

Ambadevi Temple

The Ambadevi Temple in Amravati is a revered spiritual landmark dedicated to Goddess Amba, believed to be the city’s guardian deity. Known for its ancient heritage and deep cultural significance, the temple attracts thousands of devotees, especially during festivals like Navratri. With its peaceful atmosphere and traditional architecture, it offers a serene space for prayer and reflection. Located in the heart of Amravati city, it is a must-visit for those seeking spiritual connection and cultural heritage.

4

Chhatri Talao

The Chhatri Talao in Amravati is a scenic water reservoir originally built to provide drinking water to the city. Surrounded by lush greenery and a calm atmosphere, it is now a popular spot for picnics, evening walks, and photography. The reflection of the sky and surrounding trees on the lake creates a peaceful and refreshing escape from city life. Located near Camp area, Amravati, it’s an ideal place for families, nature lovers, and those seeking relaxation by the water.

5

Wadali Talao & Wadali Garden

The Wadali Talao in Amravati is a serene lake built to supply fresh water to the city and now serves as a popular recreational spot. Adjacent to it lies the Wadali Garden, a beautifully maintained park ideal for family outings, morning walks, and children’s playtime. Together, the lake and garden create a refreshing escape with scenic views, greenery, and a peaceful environment. Located near Wadali Camp, Amravati, this destination is perfect for families, fitness enthusiasts, and nature lovers seeking relaxation close to the city.
